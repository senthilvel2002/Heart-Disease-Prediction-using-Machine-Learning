Heart Disease Prediction - Abstract

The early detection of heart disease plays a crucial role in reducing mortality rates. With advancements in machine learning, predictive models can now analyze patient data to assess the risk of heart disease. This project leverages state-of-the-art classification algorithms to provide accurate and reliable heart disease predictions. Utilizing real-world datasets and statistical analysis, our model aims to assist medical professionals in early diagnosis, ultimately improving patient outcomes.
